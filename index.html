I am currently a double major in Computer Science and Digital Narrative and Interactive Design (DNID) at the University of Pittsburgh. My studies allow me to blend technical proficiency in coding and software development with creative storytelling and interactive design. This interdisciplinary approach empowers me to explore how technologies such as games, virtual reality, and digital media can create engaging and meaningful user experiences.

As a Student Technical Consultant at the University, I assist students and faculty with technical troubleshooting and support. Additionally, I work as a Barista, where I've honed my customer service, multitasking, and time-management skills. My upcoming research focuses on modeling critical infrastructure resilience against compound threats like natural disasters and cyberattacks, designing adaptive system architectures for improved recovery and adaptability.

I am passionate about bridging the gap between technology and storytelling, particularly in the field of game development. My goal is to create immersive, story-driven experiences that push the boundaries of interactive media.
